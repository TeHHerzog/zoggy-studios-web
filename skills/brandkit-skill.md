# Brand Kit Guidance for Zoggy Studios

## Core Brand Idea

Zoggy Studios is a solo-led creative and technology studio.

The brand represents:
- Building things from scratch
- Crossing boundaries between games, tools, stories, and technology
- Honest, practical making over hype
- A single creator with AI-assisted reach

## Logo Direction

Current mark: bold geometric Z in a rounded square with a small spark orb.

The spark represents:
- Ideas becoming real
- The moment between concept and creation
- AI-assisted creativity

## Color Philosophy

- Studio site: warm light backgrounds suggest approachability
- Individual projects can diverge from the studio palette
- Dark Fantasy MMO: corrupted dark tones with ember/violet/cyan accents
- Technology tools: precise dark/light with blue or green accents
- Creative/experimental projects: more freedom, brighter accents

## Tagline

"Games, technology, and strange new worlds."

This positions the studio broadly without locking it to one genre.
